Chemical AssistantCandidates must have a B.Sc. with Chemistry as one of the subjects from a recognized university, along with 2 years’ experience in Chemical Analysis, Research, or intoxicating substance analysis from a Government laboratory or NABL-approved private institute, in roles such as Analyst, Jr. Analyst, Chemical Assistant, Chemist, Scientist, or Assistant Scientist.
Stenographer Grade-ICandidates must possess a Bachelor’s Degree from a recognized university or institution, have passed English Shorthand and Typing (120 wpm & 40 wpm on computer) or Hindi Shorthand and Typing (120 wpm & 35 wpm on computer), and possess at least 5 years of experience as a Stenographer in a Government, Public Sector, Autonomous body, or reputed Private firm.
Junior Laboratory AssistantCandidates must have either Class XII (Science stream) from a recognized board with 3 years’ laboratory experience or a Degree in Science from a recognized university/institution.
Book BinderCandidates must possess an ITI in Book Binding or an equivalent qualification from a recognized board or institution.
